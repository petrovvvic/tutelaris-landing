About the product

Tutelaris is a lightweight, local-first incident reporting app built for European EHS teams. Instead of weeks of training or long IT rollouts, it works out of the box. By turning natural-language prompts into standardized one-page reports, Tutelaris empowers teams to capture incidents quickly, stay compliant with EU safety regulations, and share consistent data across sites.
